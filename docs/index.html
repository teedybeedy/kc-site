The Tool

The app ingests public Reddit posts, applies time-decay weighting, dedupes near-duplicates, and extracts skills using a curated lexicon. It surfaces Top Skills & Tools, Co-occurrences (ecosystems), and a JD Gap view (paste a job spec—see what the market is actually talking about).

  • Ranked by trend score = votes/comments × time decay
  • De-duplication + quality filters to reduce noise
  • Map-Reduce summaries for fast, accurate rollups
  • Paste a job description to compare against market chatter

Why I built it

Recruiting and workforce planning need current signals. Surveys and vendor reports lag; Reddit reveals what practitioners struggle with now. This tool reduces noise and turns that stream into concise, defensible insights.
