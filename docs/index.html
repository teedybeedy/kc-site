The Tool

The app ingests public Reddit posts, applies time-decay weighting, de-dupes near-duplicates, and extracts skills via a curated lexicon. It surfaces Top Skills & Tools, Co-occurrences (ecosystems), and a JD Gap view (paste a JD—compare against market chatter).

  • Trend score = votes/comments × time decay
  • De-duplication + quality filters limit noise
  • Map-Reduce summaries for fast rollups
  • JD gap checker to align hiring signals to the market

Why I built it

People decisions need current signals. Surveys and vendor reports lag; Reddit surfaces what practitioners struggle with now. This app reduces noise and turns that firehose into concise, defensible insight.
