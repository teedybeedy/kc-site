The Tool

Pulls public Reddit posts via OAuth, filters by time and keywords, dedupes, and extracts skills with a curated lexicon. Signals are decay-weighted and summarized with charts & tables. Paste a JD to see gaps vs. what’s trending.

Open Reddit Skills Radar View on GitHub

What you’ll see

  1. Top skills & tools by decay-weighted trend score.
  2. Co-occurrence pairs revealing ecosystems.
  3. JD Gap: skills in your job spec vs. public spikes.
  4. Quality controls: rate-limit HUD, dedupe, strict vs fallback modes.

Why Reddit?

Practitioners speak frankly about tools and pain points—less polished than blogs, more current than surveys.
