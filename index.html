The Tool

It pulls public Reddit posts via OAuth, filters by time window and keywords, dedupes, and extracts skills with a curated lexicon. Signals are decay-weighted (half-life) and summarized with charts & tables. You can paste a JD to see gaps vs. what’s trending.

Open Reddit Skills Radar

What you’ll see

  1. Top skills & tools by decay-weighted trend score.
  2. Co-occurrence pairs that reveal ecosystems (e.g., DuckDB ↔ Polars).
  3. JD Gap: skills in your job spec vs. what’s spiking publicly.
  4. Quality controls: rate-limit HUD, dedupe, strict vs fallback modes.

Why Reddit?

It’s where practitioners speak frankly about tools and pain points—less polished than blogs, more current than surveys.

Inspired by the structure used on bettycherif.com (custom app + narrative context + CTA).

Stack
